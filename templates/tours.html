Location

Tour Duration

8 days and 7 nights exploring the rich history and stunning coastline of Malta.

Hotel Type

Boat trips to the Blue Lagoon in Comino and traditional fishing villages. Experience Maltese cuisine with included dinners at local restaurants.

Preikestolen Adventure Tour

×

Location

Preikestolen (Pulpit Rock) is a steep cliff which rises 604 metres above Lysefjorden in Forsand, Ryfylke, Norway. The top of the cliff is approximately 25 by 25 metres, almost flat, and is a famous tourist attraction in Norway.

Tour Duration

5 days and 4 nights of breathtaking Norwegian nature and adventure.

Hotel Type

Comfortable 3-star hotels and mountain lodges with stunning fjord views and authentic Norwegian hospitality.

Pricing

Within Europe: €1,200 per person

Central Asia: €1,500 per person

Other regions: Please contact for customized pricing

Tour Summary

Airport pickup from Stavanger included. Guided hike to Preikestolen with experienced local guides. Fjord cruise on Lysefjorden. Visit to Norwegian Petroleum Museum. All breakfasts and 3 dinners included. Optional activities: kayaking in the fjords, visiting traditional Norwegian villages, and experiencing the midnight sun (seasonal).

Hotel Type
